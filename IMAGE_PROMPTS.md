## Technical Specs for All Images:

### Required Dimensions:
- **Resolution**: 3840 x 2560 pixels (3:2 aspect ratio, 4K quality)
- **Alternative**: 2560 x 1707 pixels (minimum for 3:2 at high quality)
- **Why 3:2**: Perfect for full-screen backgrounds, no stretching/tearing
- **File Size**: Aim for 2-5MB per image (compressed but high quality)

### Quality Requirements:
- **Format**: PNG (for gradients) or high-quality JPEG (95%+ quality)
- **Color Depth**: 24-bit minimum
- **Gradient Quality**: Smooth, no banding or artifacts
- **Compression**: Use tools like TinyPNG after generation to optimize
- **Consistency**: Similar lighting style, smooth color transitions between images
